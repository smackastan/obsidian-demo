# Obsidian Overview

Obsidian is a specialized note taking software with beta testing starting in May of 2020 and the first official release in October of 2022. The developers estimate that they have accumulated over a million users in that time. The application supports almost every platform. It relies primarily on donations and subscribers of Obsidian Sync, their cloud storage service, to pay for operating costs.
